### 🧭 MPU-6050 Overview

It combines:

- **3-axis accelerometer** → detects gravity direction → gives pitch & roll.
    
- **3-axis gyroscope** → detects angular velocity → gives rotation rate (yaw, pitch, roll).
    

This gives you **6-DOF orientation data**, which is enough for basic attitude determination.
